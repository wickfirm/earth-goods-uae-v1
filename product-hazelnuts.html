The Story

Grown in the hazelnut
capital of the world

Ortahisar sits in the hills of Turkey's Black Sea coast — the region responsible for nearly 70% of the world's hazelnut supply. But most of that harvest ends up in industrial chocolate factories. We wanted better.

So we went to the source. These hazelnuts come from a small family orchard first planted in 1954 by Ahmet's grandfather. Four generations later, they're still hand-harvested every autumn, sun-dried on woven mats, and shelled in small batches — never industrially processed.

The result? A hazelnut with a richer, deeper flavour than anything mass-market. Buttery, slightly sweet, with that unmistakable toasted note — even though we never apply a drop of heat. That's what real hazelnuts taste like.

Common Questions

About these hazelnuts

Are these hazelnuts raw or roasted?

These are 100% raw and unroasted. Sun-dried on woven mats at the farm, shelled by hand, and shipped directly — no heat treatment at any stage. This preserves the natural fats, vitamin E and delicate flavour compounds.

If you prefer roasted hazelnuts, simply bake them at 160°C for 12–15 minutes at home. You'll get a far better result than any commercial roast.

What do these taste like compared to supermarket hazelnuts?

Richer, deeper, more complex. Most supermarket hazelnuts come from industrial processors who blend nuts from dozens of sources, then steam-roast and oil-coat them for shelf-life. You lose the nuance of the individual farm.

Our hazelnuts come from a single origin, hand-harvested in one season, sun-dried naturally. The result: a buttery, slightly sweet flavour with gentle toasted notes — even raw. Customers often describe them as "the first time I really tasted a hazelnut."

Can I use these to make homemade hazelnut butter?

Absolutely — they're ideal. For smooth butter: lightly toast at 160°C for 10 minutes (the oils release better), then blend in a food processor for 8–10 minutes, scraping down the sides, until creamy. No oil needed — the natural fats do the work. Add a pinch of sea salt and a spoon of honey if you like.

Are these safe for pregnancy and kids?

Raw organic hazelnuts are safe and nutritious for pregnancy and children over 4 years old (whole nuts are a choking hazard for younger kids — grind or blend them first). They're a fantastic source of folate, vitamin E, and healthy fats that support fetal brain development.

Always check with your doctor if you have specific dietary restrictions or nut allergies in your family.

How long do they stay fresh?

Unopened pouch: 12 months in a cool, dry cupboard. Opened: transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 6 months — the fridge dramatically extends freshness in the UAE climate. You can also freeze them for up to 12 months with no loss of quality.
