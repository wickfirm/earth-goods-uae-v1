Good to Know

Raw Nuts FAQ

Everything you need to know about our certified organic raw nuts — from storage to sourcing.

Are these nuts raw or roasted?

All nuts in this collection are 100% raw and unroasted. We never heat-treat, oil-roast or salt our nuts. This preserves the natural fats, vitamin E and delicate enzymes that roasting can damage — and gives you full control to roast at home if you prefer.

How should I store raw nuts?

Raw nuts contain natural oils that can go rancid if exposed to heat, light or air for too long. Once opened, we recommend transferring to an airtight container and keeping them in a cool, dark cupboard — or better still, in the fridge or freezer for up to 6 months.

UAE climate tip: the fridge is your friend. Cold storage keeps raw nuts fresh, crunchy and full-flavoured far longer than pantry storage.

Where do your nuts come from?

Every nut in our range is sourced directly from certified organic farms. Hazelnuts from Ortahisar, Turkey. Almonds from Andalucía, Spain. Walnuts from Umbria, Italy. Cashews from fair-trade cooperatives in Kerala, India. We work with the same growers year after year and visit every farm in person before adding a product to our range.

Are these suitable for people with nut allergies?

No — these are tree nuts and are not safe for anyone with a tree-nut allergy. Our products are also packaged in a facility that handles other tree nuts and sesame, so there is a risk of cross-contact. If you have a severe allergy, please consult your healthcare provider before consuming.

Can I soak these nuts for activated snacking?

Yes — because our nuts are raw and unroasted, they're perfect for soaking. Cover with filtered water and a pinch of sea salt, soak for 8–12 hours, then drain and rinse. Soaked nuts are easier to digest and have a softer, creamier texture — ideal for smoothies, nut-milks, or homemade cheeses.

Do you offer bulk or family-size packs?

Yes — most of our raw nuts are available in 200g, 500g and 1kg sizes. The larger the pack, the better the per-kilo value. If you're interested in even larger bulk orders for families, offices or wellness studios, email us at hello@earthgoods.com for custom pricing.
